Understanding "Total Annual Payments"

The Definition

Think of this as the city's "Mortgage Payment." It combines the principal repayment (paying back the loan) plus the interest for all tracked projects combined.

What it Means for You

This is the extra cash the City must find in the budget every single year for 30 years just to pay for these buildings. This money cannot be used for parks, roads, or other services.

The Bottom Line

It is the "Price of Admission" for these projects, expressed as an annual bill to the taxpayer.
